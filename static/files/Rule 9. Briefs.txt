Arizona Revised Statutes Annotated 
Rules of Procedure for Direct Appeals from Decisions of the Corporation Commission to the Arizona Court of Appeals (Refs & Annos)
17B A.R.S. Rules Corporation Comm.Appeals, Rule 9
Rule 9. Briefs
Currentness
The provisions of Rules 13 and 14, ARCAP, apply to direct appeals of Commission decisions, except that the brief of the appellant shall, in its statement of the case, and in addition to all other matters required, state the disposition of the administrative proceeding by the Commission.
Credits
Added Dec. 12, 1991, effective Dec. 31, 1991. Amended Jan. 24, 2001, effective June 1, 2001.
