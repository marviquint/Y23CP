Arizona Revised Statutes Annotated 
Rules of Procedure for Direct Appeals from Decisions of the Corporation Commission to the Arizona Court of Appeals (Refs & Annos)
17B A.R.S. Rules Corporation Comm.Appeals, Rule 3
Rule 3. Shortening or Extension of Time
Currentness
The provisions of Rule 5, ARCAP, are applicable to direct appeals of Commission decisions, except that the time periods set forth in A.R.S. §§ 40-253 and 40-254.01 may not be shortened or extended.
Credits
Added Dec. 12, 1991, effective Dec. 31, 1991.
